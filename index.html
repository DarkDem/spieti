SPIETI: Our Home

The Techno India SPIE Student Chapter is an official student group, comprising students of various engineering disciplines at Techno India, Salt Lake. The chapter is affiliated to SPIE(The International Society for Optics and Photonics). Our chapter is based in the Department of Electronics and Instrumentation, Techno India, Salt Lake. The primary goal of our chapter is to study about the latest advancements in optics/photonics, and at the same time enlighten people about the fascinating world of Light and Light based technologies.
